FAQ

Questions, Answered

Is this just a chatbot?

No. A chatbot only replies to messages. OmniConvo is a complete growth system — it captures leads from WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook and WebChat, qualifies them with AI, logs them into a CRM with pipeline tracking, runs automated follow-ups, AND manages your social media content and engagement. The whole inquiry-to-sale process runs without manual work.

How does AI social media management actually work?

Our AI content engine drafts posts, Reels scripts, and captions in your brand voice. Our human team refines designs and strategy. You approve everything through a simple approval flow before it goes live. AI + human team then handles comments and DMs — routing buying-intent messages straight into your CRM as leads.

Will automation get my WhatsApp number banned?

No — that's the point of using the official WhatsApp Business API. Unofficial bulk-messaging tools frequently get numbers banned. We set you up on the Meta-approved API, so your messaging stays compliant and stable.

Do I lose control of my pages?

Never. You retain full ownership and admin access to every page. We work as authorized managers, every post is approved by you before publishing, and you can pause or adjust anything at any time.

Do you work with businesses outside Pakistan?

Yes. We're based in Karachi and serve clients across Pakistan and internationally. The system works the same way globally, and our team supports clients in both English and Urdu.

How long does it take to go live?

Basic automation can go live within hours. A full setup with custom flows and your first content calendar typically takes 5-10 days, depending on complexity.

What does it cost?

Automation plans start at Rs 4,999/month, social media management at Rs 24,999/month, and bundles save you up to Rs 6,000/month. A one-time implementation service starts from Rs 34,999. AI API usage is billed separately by the provider.
